The problem

You want to send a message to multiple contacts and want an answer from each of them? Then sending emails via CC or BCC is not the best choice.

  • When sending an email with all contacts in CC, all contacts are visible to everybody. Many people will simply not respond as they hide in the mass. "Not-me" or "Somebody-else" syndrome.
  • When sending an email with all contacts in BCC, each recepient receives an impersonal email. People identify such emails as SPAM or unfriendly. No salutation at all is rude.
Strongly solves that problem for you.
